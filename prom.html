Prom Outfits

What is prom?
Prom is a high school event in the spring of senior year. It gives seniors a chance to make some memories with their friends and classmates before high school ends and everyone goes their own ways.
What could one wear to prom?
Usually it depends on the theme (if your school has set one). Otherwise, You should try to look elegant but minimal. In the end, it's your prom and you only get it once; It's completely upto you what you want to wear.
